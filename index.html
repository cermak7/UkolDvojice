About Us

Leigh McAdam is a Calgary-based writer, photographer and social media enthusiast with over 48,000 followers. Her blog: HikeBikeTravel is frequently cited as one of the top travel and outdoor adventure blogs in Canada and consistently receives over 135,000 monthly page views.
